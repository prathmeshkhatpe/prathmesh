About Us

Nestled in the lush landscapes of Bhor, Hotel Raireshwar Garden offers a peaceful retreat for nature lovers and trekkers. Located close to the historic Raireshwar Temple, our hotel combines comfort with cultural richness.

Nearby Attractions

  • Raireshwar Temple – Historic site of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj’s oath
  • Kenjalgad Fort – Scenic trekking destination
  • Gomukh and Aswal Lakes – Perfect for nature walks
  • Pandav Leni – Ancient Buddhist caves
